Ride More, Pay Less

Effective May 1, 2023

Cash Fare: $4.50

Exact Change required when paying by Cash. If requested, a two-hour paper will be transfered at the time of payment. This transfer will allow you to transfer to any Brampton Transit Route (Including Route 41 - Bolton) within two-hours

Contactless Credit Card and Debit Card: $4.50

When paying with a contactless credit or debit card, including via phone or watch using Google Wallet or Apple Wallet, a two-hour transfer is automatically included in the fare.
