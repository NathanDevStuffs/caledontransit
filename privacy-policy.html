Privacy Policy

Effective: September 3, 2025 • Last Updated: September 3, 2025

Caledon Transit (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates https://caledontransit.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data — If you enable location services, we may collect your approximate or precise location to show nearby transit options and features.
  • Analytics Data — We automatically collect information about how you interact with our site (pages viewed, clicks, session duration, device/browser details).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide maps, routes, and location-based transit features.
  • Improve functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • Monitor uptime, detect, diagnose, and fix errors.
  • Understand usage patterns and trends.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Privacy Policy
  • TomTom API — Privacy Policy
  • Hotjar — Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated or de-identified data with third parties for analytics and service improvements.

10. Contact Us

  • If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@caledontransit.com
